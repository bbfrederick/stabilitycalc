Ghost ROI Analysis

This is an analysis of the amplitude and time variation of image ghosts. Odd and even ghosts are assessed by calculating the ratio of the average signal in a ${ghostroisize}x${ghostroisize}x1 ghost roi to the average amplitude in the center of the phantom. The ghost roi is placed at the edge of the field of view in the phase encode direction, outside the phantom, and in the center (even ghost) or at the edge (odd ghost) of the phantom position in the readout direction.
