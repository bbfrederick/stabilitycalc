Maximum sensitivity region ROI Analysis

This is an analysis of the temporal fluctuation in the ROI of maximum sensitivity for the individual coil element. A 3 x 3 x 1 voxel is automatically positioned at the center of gravity, and the average voxel value is plotted a function of time. Linear and quadratic terms are fit to the drift. The voxel statistics are reported with and without the drift removed.

Raw max sensitivity ROI plot

mean=${'%4.4f' % maxlocmean}
stddev=${'%4.4f' % maxlocstddev} ${maxloc_qualitytag}
p-p=${'%4.4f' % maxlocpp} ${maxloc_pp_qualitytag}

Detrended max sensitivity ROI plot

mean=${'%4.4f' % maxlocmean_dt}
stddev=${'%4.4f' % maxlocstddev_dt} ${maxloc_dt_qualitytag}
p-p=${'%4.4f' % maxlocpp_dt} ${maxlocpp_dt_qualitytag}
