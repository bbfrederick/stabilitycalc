Central ROI Analysis

This is an analysis of the temporal fluctuation in a central voxels. A ${centralroisize}x${centralroisize}x1 voxel is automatically positioned at the center of gravity, and the average voxel value is plotted a function of time. Linear and quadratic terms are fit to the drift. The voxel statistics are reported with and without the drift removed.

Raw central ROI plot

mean=${centmean}
stddev=${centstddev} ${centstddev_qualitytag}
p-p=5.3300 ${centpp_qualitytag}
drift=${centdrift}

Detrended central ROI plot

mean=${centmean_dt}
stddev=${centstddev_dt} ${centstddev_dt_qualitytag}
p-p=${centpp_dt} ${centpp_dt_qualitytag}
