Peripheral ROI Analysis

This is an analysis of the image intensity and SFNR variation in a set of ${peripheralroisize}x${peripheralroisize}x1 voxels at a fixed radius from the center of the object in the central axial slice (${peripheralradfrac} of the distance from the center to the edge of the phantom). For phased array coils this is likely to have better signal to noise than an ROI at the center of the coil. The variation in SNR as a function of angle is also displayed.

Raw peripheral ROI plot

mean=${'%4.4f' % periphmean}
stddev=${'%4.4f' % periphstddev} ${periph_qualitytag}
p-p=${'%4.4f' % periphpp} ${periphpp_qualitytag}
drift=${'%4.4f' % periphdrift}

mean=${'%4.4f' % periphmean_dt}
